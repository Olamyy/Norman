Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most asked question about Norman..
  • 1.What exactly is Norman?

    Norman is an artificial intelligence powered platform that helps medical facilities(hospitals) keep track and provide ready to use medical assistance to their patients straight from social media.

  • 2.What social media platforms are supported now?

    Norman currently only works with the facebook messenger. However, the team behind Norman is continuously working to expand so it will in no time expand to all other platforms.

  • 3.Is the platform only for hospitals?

    The platform majorly relies on the patient(user) being registered to an hospital. As such, a use can only enjoy the best of the platform only if he/she is registered to a hospital. However, Norman has some other awesome medical related features for users that aren't registered to a hospital.
