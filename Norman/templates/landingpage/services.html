Norman Services

Norman currently offers the following services.
Reminder

This service handles all reminder related events. It tries to unburden your patient from excessively remembering some things by providing the following features:

  • Drug use Reminder
  • Appointment Reminders
  • User Specific Reminders
Appointment/
Scheduling

This service allows patients to fix appointment at hospital.

Real-Time
Messaging

This service provides a real time messaging interface between your patient's social media platform and you. You can get in real time all messages a user sends in your registered email and also your Norman Dashboard.


Inventory

This services allows hospital keep track of patients registered under them.

Emergency

The emergency service handles all emergency related activities. It helps by providing a platform for:

  • Patients to alert hospitals in times of emergencies.
  • Patients to get emergency real-time help from their hospitals(if its available).
  • Hospitals to serve patients better
